Rules - Cube Kingdom - 1.21 SMP

By using the services of Cube Kingdom, this set of rules is automatically accepted and every person who uses our services must comply with it without exception.

#1 General rule

  1. The following rules may be changed by us at any time and without prior notice. Each player must inform himself about changes.
  2. If a violation is found that is not listed in this rulebook, but clearly violates the Minecraft EULA or is obviously contrary to the purpose of a thing (e.g. an in-game game), it can also be punished.
  3. The instructions of team members must be followed and respected! Anyone who does not follow the instructions of a moderator, admin or owner can be punished!
  4. Ignorance does not protect against punishment!
  5. Friendliness comes first. Nobody needs arguments.
    5.2 Avatars, skins or similar may not have any offensive or insulting content.
    5.3 Any kind of spam (e.g. sending the same message several times or sending an unnecessary and long message without content (e.g.: "hfef74z874HR(/§Z(h8t93...")) is prohibited.
  6. Personal data remains personal! It is strictly forbidden to pass on telephone numbers, e-mail addresses, addresses, passwords or similar to others without permission! Sensitive data will not be requested from a team member at any time.
  7. The voice or even just the face of another person using our network may not be made accessible (published) to the public without that person's consent.
    7.2 In principle, streaming is still permitted. However, this should always be agreed in advance with the players in a group (for example: through Simple Voice Mod)!
  8. Bypassing bans is prohibited!

#2 Behavior in the chat

  1. Misleading, offensive or insulting content (e.g. in chats, on posters, on buildings,...) is strictly forbidden! This includes sexual innuendos, offensive content, right-wing extremist codes or slogans, provocative innuendos and the like. An initial assessment as to whether something is inappropriate or not will be made by a moderator / admin / owner.
  2. Cheating, phishing etc. are prohibited.

#3 In-game rules

  1. Harming other people who play on our Minecraft network (or Minecraft server) so that they lose the fun of the game is prohibited.
  2. Exploiting bugs in the game is prohibited. Bugs can be reported at any time on our Discord server.
  3. Client modifications that create an unfair advantage in the game (e.g. Killaura, X-Ray, ...) are prohibited. Even entering the network with such an unauthorized modification can result in penalties!
  4. Building inappropriate, offensive or insulting structures is prohibited.
  5. Advertising for your own store (your own "business" in the game) in the game chat is permitted, but may only be posted in the chat once every 5 minutes per store.
    6 Spawntrapping is prohibited.
  6. The use of bots or other functions that strongly automate your own game behavior is prohibited.
    7.2 The excessive use of autoclickers is also not permitted! Autoclickers may be used for the use of automatic farms. However, all autoclickers with over 40 CPS (clicks per second) are prohibited!
  7. Enough distance to the spawn must be kept (in the Nether 50 blocks, as well as in the Overworld 400 blocks).
  8. The main end island must not be destroyed or built on too much! For projects that are only possible on the main island, please discuss this with an admin first!
  9. Signs can be used to mark out areas (but there is no real claim system)! Nevertheless, these claim signs should be respected by other players!

#4 Penalties

  1. The administration team reserves the right to impose penalties at any time and without giving reasons!
  2. Ban requests can only be submitted on our Discord server.
  3. Punishments of any kind will be documented by all team members. The server team checks every case for errors. If a violation cannot be sufficiently proven, the statement of another team member will suffice.
