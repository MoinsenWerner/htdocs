Cube Kingdom

Cube Kingdom is a German Minecraft server that welcomes everyone!
 Our goal is to offer a versatile gaming experience that is available to everyone and creates space for the passion and creativity of each individual member. 
But that's not all - our community takes center stage!
Every member has the opportunity to submit project proposals and actively vote on them.
This is how we shape the future of our server together.

Large community

Through our Discord server, we have a pleasant community, so you almost never have to play alone!

Experience

Because we have already hosted a large number of projects, we have gained a lot of experience, which we always incorporate into our projects!

Own server

We host all our projects on our own servers!
