Rules - Cube Kingdom

 

English Version --> Rules <--

 

Durch das Nutzen der Dienste von Cube Kingdom wird dieses Regelwerk automatisch akzeptiert und jede Person, die unsere Dienste in Anspruch nimmt, hat sich ohne Ausnahmen an dieses zu halten.

#1 Allgemeine Regel

  1. The following rules may be changed by us at any time and without prior notice. All players must inform themselves of any changes.
  2. If a violation is detected that is not listed in these rules, but clearly violates the Minecraft EULA or is obviously contrary to the purpose of a matter (e.g. an in-game game), this can also be penalized.
  3. The instructions of the team members must be followed and respected! Anyone who does not follow the instructions of a moderator, admin or owner can be punished!
  4. Ignorance is no defense against punishment!
  5. Freundlichkeit steht an erster Stelle. Niemand braucht Streit.
    5.2. Avatare, Skins oder ähnliches dürfen keinen anstößigen oder beleidigenden Inhalt haben.
    5.3. Jegliche Art von Spam (beispielsweise mehrmaliges schicken der selben Nachricht oder senden einer unnötigen und langen Nachricht ohne Inhalt (z.B.: “hfef74z874HR(/§Z(h8t93...")) ist verboten.
  6. Personal data remains personal! It is strictly forbidden to pass on telephone numbers, e-mail addresses, addresses, passwords or similar to others without permission! Sensitive data will never be requested requested by a team member.
  7. The voice or even just the face of another person using our network may not be made accessible (published) to the public without that person's consent.
    7.2 Streaming is generally allowed anyway. nevertheless permitted. However, this should always be agreed beforehand with the players in a group (for example: through Simple Voice Mod)!
  8. Bypassing bans is prohibited!

#2 Behavior in the chat

  1. Misleading, offensive or insulting content (e.g. in chats, on posters, on buildings, etc.) is strictly prohibited! This includes sexual innuendos, offensive content, right-wing extremist codes or slogans, provocative innuendos and similar. slogans, provocative innuendos and the like. An initial assessment of whether something is inappropriate or not will be made by a moderator / admin / owner.
  2. Fraud, phishing and the like are prohibited.

#3 Ingame-Rules

  1. Harming other players on our Minecraft network (or Minecraft server) to the point that they lose enjoyment of the game is prohibited.
  2. Exploiting bugs in the game is prohibited. Bugs can be reported at any time on our Discord server.
  3. Client modifications that provide an unfair gameplay advantage (e.g., Killaura, X-Ray, ...) are prohibited. Even entering the network with such an unauthorized modification can result in penalties!
  4. Building inappropriate, offensive, or insulting structures is prohibited.
  5. Advertising for your own shop (personal "business" in the game) in the game chat is allowed but may only be posted once every 5 minutes per shop.
  6. Spawn trapping is prohibited.
  7. The use of bots or other functions that heavily automate your gameplay is prohibited.
    7.2 The excessive use of autoclickers is also not desired! Autoclickers may be used for automatic farms. However, all autoclickers with over 40 CPS (clicks per second) are prohibited!
  8. A sufficient distance from the spawn must be maintained (50 blocks in the Nether, as well as 400 blocks in the Overworld).
  9. The main End island must not be excessively destroyed or built upon! For projects that can only be done on the main island, please discuss this with an admin first!
  10. Signs can be used to mark areas (there is no proper claim system)! Nevertheless, these claim signs should be respected by other players!

#4 Penalties

  1. The administration team reserves the right to impose penalties at any time and without giving reasons!
  2. Appeals for bans can only be submitted on our Discord server.
  3. Penalties of any kind are documented by all team members. The server team reviews each case for errors. If a violation is not sufficiently provable, the statement of another team member is also sufficient.

Cube Kingdom

Cube Kingdom is a German Minecraft community server that offers a wide variety of projects. From vanilla servers to PvP projects, everything is included.
